### What is RTO AI Automation?

RTO AI automation is software that uses artificial intelligence to handle student enrollment calls — without human intervention for the routine questions.

The AI answers calls, qualifies inquiries, collects information (including USI numbers), books orientations, and sends SMS confirmations. When it hits something it can't handle, it transfers to your staff with context.

**What RTO AI automation handles:**
- Course inquiries (pricing, duration, entry requirements)
- Orientation scheduling (booking, rescheduling, reminders)
- USI collection and verification
- Funding eligibility questions (basic)
- Enrollment status inquiries
- Basic policy questions (refund, deferral)

**What it doesn't handle:**
- Complex funding disputes
- Complaints and grievances
- Legal or regulatory exceptions
- Conversations requiring judgment

The key distinction: RTO AI handles the calls your staff dread — the repetitive, time-consuming inquiries. Your team handles the calls that matter — complex enrollments, students who need extra support, high-value conversations.

### How Does RTO AI Automation Work?

Here's the typical flow for an AI-powered enrollment call:

**1. Call comes in (24/7, no wait)**
Student calls your RTO number. AI answers within 2 rings.

> "Thank you for calling [RTO Name]. This call is being recorded for training and quality assurance purposes. How can I help you today?"

**2. AI qualifies the inquiry**
AI asks a few quick questions to understand what the caller needs.

> "Are you interested in enrolling in a course today?"

> "Do you have your USI number handy, or would you like help finding it?"

**3. AI collects required information**
AI gathers name, contact, course interest, USI, and other enrollment details.

**4. AI books orientation**
AI offers available orientation slots and books the student automatically.

> "We have orientations available on July 15 at 10am or July 22 at 2pm. Which works for you?"

**5. SMS confirmation sent**
Student receives immediate SMS with orientation details and confirmation request.

**6. Human handoff (if needed)**
If AI can't answer the question, it transfers to staff with full context:

> "I'm going to connect you with a team member who can help with your funding question. They'll have your details already."

### Data Flywheel Mechanics

**What is a data flywheel**:
A data flywheel is when your product creates value from data, which attracts more users, which creates more data, which makes the product more valuable. It self-reinforces.

**Classic examples**:
| Company | Flywheel | Result |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Salesforce | More users → more CRM data → better predictions → more users | Market leader |
| HubSpot | More users → more marketing data → better tools → more users | $25B+ valuation |
| ServiceNow | More workflows → more enterprise data → better automation → more workflows | Market leader |

**Optimizer AI's data flywheel**:
```
1. RTO uses AI → AI handles calls → call data collected
2. Call data + outcomes → AI model improves → higher containment rate
3. Higher containment → better ROI → RTO refers other RTOs
4. More RTOs → more call data → AI improves faster
5. Go to step 2
```

**The loop in detail**:

| Step | Input | Process | Output | Loop trigger |
|------|-------|---------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | RTO onboards | AI handles calls | Call recording + transcript | Data collection |
| 2 | Call data | Analyze for patterns | Containment rate, escalation reasons | AI improvement |
| 3 | Better AI | Handles more calls | Higher ROI | Customer retention |
| 4 | Happy customer | Refers peers | New RTO onboarded | New customer |
| 5 | New RTO | More calls | More data | Back to step 2 |

**Flywheel speed factors**:
- Number of RTOs (more RTOs = faster data collection)
- Call volume per RTO (high-volume RTOs = more data faster)
- Feedback quality (did the call result in enrollment?)
- Time to iterate (how fast can Kham update AI based on data)
