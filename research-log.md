### Deal Stall Triggers (And Prevention)

**Trigger 1: "I need to talk to my business partner"**:
- Problem: Not the decision-maker, or decision requires partner approval
- Frequency: 25-30% of medium/large RTOs
- Prevention: Ask in discovery "Who else is involved in this decision?"
- Recovery: "Can I help prepare your pitch for them?"

**Trigger 2: "Let's revisit after [funding cycle]"**:
- Problem: Timing (Q4 budget locked, waiting for new fiscal)
- Frequency: 20-25% of all RTOs
- Prevention: Ask "When does your funding cycle reset?"
- Recovery: "Let's schedule 2 weeks before that so you're ready"

**Trigger 3: "My staff won't use it"**:
- Problem: Change resistance from enrollment staff
- Frequency: 15-20% of RTOs
- Prevention: "Staff love it — let me show you what they say"
- Recovery: "Can I bring your enrollment manager to the next demo?"

**Trigger 4: "We tried AI before, it didn't work"**:
- Problem: Negative past experience (generic tool failure)
- Frequency: 10-15% of RTOs
- Prevention: "Built for ASQA compliance — that's different"
- Recovery: "What specifically failed? We handle that."

**Trigger 5: "Your price is higher than I expected"**:
- Problem: Budget alignment
- Frequency: 20-25% of RTOs
- Prevention: Anchor on ROI early ("It's $999/mo, saves you $12K")
- Recovery: "What if we showed you the ROI first?"

**Trigger 6: "I need more time to think"**:
- Problem: Analysis paralysis
- Frequency: 30-40% of RTOs
- Prevention: Set decision deadline in demo
- Recovery: "What specifically do you need to decide?"

### Deal Velocity Optimization Framework

**Step 1: Classify prospects by velocity**:
| Signal | Velocity class | Expected timeline |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Staff just left, CEO calling us | High | 14-21 days |
| CEO has full authority, no partners | High | 14-21 days |
| ASQA audit in <2 months | High | 21-30 days |
| Medium RTO, CEO + partner | Medium | 30-45 days |
| Large RTO, board approval | Low | 60-90 days |
| "General interest, no urgency" | Low | 90+ days or never |

**Step 2: Prioritize high-velocity**:
- High velocity: Call today, demo this week, proposal by end of week
- Medium velocity: Call this week, demo next week, proposal in 2 weeks
- Low velocity: Add to nurture, follow up in 3 months

**Step 3: Accelerate medium-velocity**:
- Add urgency: "January is when call volume spikes. Want to be ready?"
- Add trust: "I can connect you with [similar RTO] who went live in 8 weeks"
- Add ROI: "Let me calculate the exact ROI for your call volume"

**Step 4: Nurture or disqualify low-velocity**:
- Nurture: Monthly email, follow up in 3 months
- Disqualify: Remove from active pipeline if no response in 90 days
