### Channel Attribution Framework

**Attribution model: First-touch + Last-touch hybrid**

- **First-touch credit**: Channel where lead first engaged (awareness)
- **Last-touch credit**: Channel where lead converted (closing)
- **Multi-touch credit**: Shared across touchpoints (for complex journeys)

**For Optimizer AI (B2B SaaS), use:**

| Scenario | Attribution | Rationale |
|----------|-------------|-----------|

| Content → demo | Content (100%) | Content drove intent |
| LinkedIn → content → demo | Content (60%), LinkedIn (40%) | Content close, LinkedIn aware |
| Partner → LinkedIn → demo | Partner (60%), LinkedIn (40%) | Partner close, LinkedIn nurture |

**Channel ROI formula:**

```
Channel ROI = (Revenue from channel - Cost of channel) / Cost of channel

Example:
- Partner channel cost: $800/month
- Partner customers: 2/month × $999 = $1,998/month
- Partner channel revenue (annual): $1,998 × 12 = $23,976
- Partner channel ROI: ($23,976 - $800) / $800 = 2,897% (annual)
```
